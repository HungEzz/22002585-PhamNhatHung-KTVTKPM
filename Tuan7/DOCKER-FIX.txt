Loi: "docker daemon is not running" / "pipe/docker_engine ... not found"

Cach 1 (khuyen nghi):
  - Mo "Docker Desktop" tren Windows.
  - Doi icon ca voi chuyen sang trang thai "Running".
  - Chay lai (tu thu muc goc buoi6): docker compose up -d

Cach 2 (khong dung Docker):
  - Cai RabbitMQ cho Windows (can Erlang + RabbitMQ), chay broker tren cong 5672, user/pass guest/guest.

Cach 3 (RabbitMQ may khac):
  - Dat bien moi truong: RABBITMQ_HOST, RABBITMQ_PORT, RABBITMQ_USER, RABBITMQ_PASS
  - Vi du: set RABBITMQ_HOST=192.168.1.10
